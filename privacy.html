Privacy Policy

How BB369TECH Handles Website Information

This policy explains how information submitted through the BB369TECH corporate website and official communication channels may be collected, used, shared, protected, and retained.

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Policy Sections Scope Information We Collect How Information Is Used Corporate Contact Forms Platform Requests Service Providers Information Sharing Data Retention Security Choices and Requests Policy Updates Contact

Please do not submit passwords, payment card information, government identification numbers, medical information, or other highly sensitive personal information through the corporate contact form.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the BB369TECH corporate website at https://bb369tech.com and information submitted through its public contact forms and official communication channels.

Separate websites, operating platforms, service providers, and third-party systems may have additional notices or policies that apply to their own activities.

2. Information We May Collect

BB369TECH may collect information that visitors voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name and company name
  • Email address
  • Country or region
  • Inquiry category
  • Message content
  • Information included in submitted forms

Technical systems may also process limited information such as IP address, browser type, device information, access time, referring page, and basic security logs.

3. How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to:

  • Review and respond to business inquiries
  • Evaluate potential services or cooperation
  • Provide technical or platform-related support
  • Maintain website security and service continuity
  • Prevent misuse, fraud, or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect BB369TECH, its users, and participating businesses

4. Corporate Contact Forms

Information submitted through the corporate contact form may be processed by BB369TECH and its approved email, hosting, security, and infrastructure service providers for the purpose of reviewing, routing, storing, and responding to the inquiry.

Submitting a contact form does not create a contract, partnership, service commitment, or guaranteed response time.

5. Platform and Service Requests

The BB369TECH Platform is an operating platform developed and managed by BB369TECH. Information submitted through a local service request may be shared with a relevant participating service business when necessary to review or respond to that request.

Final pricing, availability, scheduling, service scope, warranties, contracts, and other service commitments require human confirmation by the relevant parties.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

BB369TECH may use third-party services for website hosting, email delivery, form processing, infrastructure, security, analytics, and other operational needs.

These providers may process information only as necessary to perform their services, subject to their own terms, privacy policies, and legal obligations.

7. Information Sharing

BB369TECH does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared when reasonably necessary to:

  • Respond to a submitted inquiry
  • Provide or support a requested service
  • Route a platform request to a relevant business
  • Operate approved website and email systems
  • Comply with law, legal process, or regulatory obligations
  • Protect rights, safety, security, or property

8. Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary for business communication, service delivery, security, legal compliance, recordkeeping, and dispute resolution.

Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the inquiry, operational requirements, and applicable law.

9. Security

BB369TECH may use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect submitted information. However, no internet transmission, email service, or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Choices and Information Requests

Visitors may contact BB369TECH to request correction, clarification, or deletion of information they previously submitted, subject to applicable law, security requirements, and legitimate recordkeeping needs.

11. External Links

The website may link to third-party websites and services. BB369TECH does not control and is not responsible for their content, security, availability, or privacy practices.

12. Children

The corporate website is intended for business and general informational use. BB369TECH does not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children through the corporate contact form without appropriate authorization.

13. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated when website features, services, legal requirements, or business practices change. The effective date will be revised when appropriate.

14. Contact

Questions or requests concerning this Privacy Policy should be submitted through the official contact page.
